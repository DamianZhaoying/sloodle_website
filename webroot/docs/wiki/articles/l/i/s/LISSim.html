Background

To develop or enhance curricula within graduate schools of library and information science to enhance courses or programs of study related to the development of critical thinking skills, such as organization leadership and research methods. IMLS

To develop critical thinking skills regarding how to interact with specific types of patrons, manage library space effectively, deliver reference services, etc.
