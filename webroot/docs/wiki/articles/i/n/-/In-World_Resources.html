In-World Resources

From SLIS Second Life Wiki

  • College of Scripting, Music and Science

Get the basics on the first floor - learn how to move, talk, and manage your inventory. By the time you reach the fourth floor, you’ll have learned some advanced scripting. The instructions here are step-by-step and easy to read.



  • Ivory Tower Library of Primitives

Have you seen something that has inspired you? Do you want to learn how to create your own in-world objects? The Ivory Tower of Prims provides step-by-step building instructions in several languages.



  • ICT Library: Information Communications & Tech. for Educators

Once you’ve learned some basic building techniques, learn how to make your objects interactive. You can pick up all sorts of scripts and ideas here. (This library is located on Info Island - a great place to learn about libraries in SL.)
