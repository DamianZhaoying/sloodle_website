L3Q9

"You can send an IM to any person you have a calling card for. Locate and select their calling card from your Inventory. Double-click on the card to view their profile. Then click on the Instant Message button on their profile Second Life tab."


You can send an IM to any person you have a ___________ card for.

A) calling

B) reply

C) avatar

D) inventory


L3Q10

"You can IM someone who is offline. They will see the IM when they log back into Second Life or sooner if they have it set up so that IMs get sent to their email. And there's more, if they reply to the email you will receive the IM in Second Life instantly."


You can IM someone who is offline. They will see the IM when they log back into Second Life or sooner if they have it set up so that IMs get sent to their _________.

A) email

B) Pop-Up

C) Home

D) Avatar


L3Q11

"Click on the Groups tab in the Communicate window. If you are part of a group in Second Life, your group will be listed there. Double-click on the list to send an IM to the group."


Click on the Groups tab in the Communicate window. If you are part of a group in Second Life, your group will be listed there. _______________ on the list to send an IM to the group.

A) Double-click

B) Right-click

C) Left-click

D) Click


L3Q12

"You can also create other IM groups of residents you want to communicate with on a regular basis by creating a calling cards folder for the group."


You can also create other IM groups of residents you want to communicate with on a regular basis by creating a calling cards folder for the _________.

A) Group

B) Meeting

C) Communication

D) Callers


L3Q13

"You can start an IM session with all the residents in a folder by R-clicking (Cmd-click) on the group's folder, then selecting Start Conference Chat from the drop down menu. Start Conference Chat opens the conversation with everyone in your folder who is currently online."


You can start an IM session with all the residents in a folder by R-clicking (Cmd-click) on the group's folder, then selecting Start ________________ Chat from the drop down menu.

A) Conference

B) Calling

C) Group

D) Avatar
